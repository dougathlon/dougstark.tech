Agentic Humanities Lab V1 is local-first research infrastructure for AI-assisted humanities work. Its design commitment is separation: sources, notes, provenance, review, and publication should not collapse into one undifferentiated stream.

The lab treats workflow as an argument. It makes judgment inspectable as materials move from private research toward reviewed public description, so AI can assist without quietly deciding what becomes public.
