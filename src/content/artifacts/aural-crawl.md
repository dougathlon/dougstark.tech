Aural Crawl is a sonic maze video game with no visuals, built in Unity in 2019. It asks players to navigate by sound alone, turning orientation into a practice of listening.

The point is not sensory deprivation as novelty. The game makes perspective audible: position, danger, and progress have to be reconstructed through timing, memory, and trust in a changing acoustic field.
