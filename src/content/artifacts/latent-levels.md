Latent Levels uses games to make AI systems available to experience. Instead of explaining tokenization, agency, or model constraint from the outside, it builds situations where those operations become playable.

Human Mode and Tokenizer Training test the same premise: computation becomes clearer when players feel where an interface invites, narrows, or misreads action.
