Platform Orthographer is a Unity game from 2021: a 3D platformer played in 2D. It asks players to act in depth while seeing and moving through an orthographic constraint.

The design makes spatial literacy unstable in a useful way. Players have to translate depth into flat movement, so perspective becomes an operation rather than a view.
