Synthesizing POV treats perspective as a designed relation, not a camera position. It asks how play changes when sight is withheld, flattened, translated, or made unreliable.

Aural Crawl removes the visual field so sonic navigation becomes primary. Platform Orthographer asks players to act inside a 3D platforming space through a 2D mode of play. Both works make point of view into a problem players must solve with their bodies.
