Askesis names the exercise side of the work: media philosophy as a practiced relation to attention, attitude, training, and bodily technique.

It remains private because the public research now travels more clearly through Gaming Habits, Untimely Play, and Synthetic Humanities Lab. The concept still matters as a way to understand thinking as something one rehearses.
