Generative AI makes old humanistic questions newly practical: how judgment forms, how style travels, how evidence is prepared, and how collaborators know when a machine has entered the situation.

Synthetic Humanities Lab builds artifacts and local infrastructure around that problem. Its wager is that AI-assisted scholarship becomes more trustworthy when sources, notes, provenance, review, and publication remain distinct public and private registers.
