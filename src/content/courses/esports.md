## Catalog Description

Esports begins from a simple premise: competitive video games are media systems, not games plus prizes. They make competition legible through interfaces, rules, broadcasts, rankings, sponsorships, training routines, and communities of practice.

The course moves through five overlapping frames: sport, design, business, entertainment, and culture/community. Students analyze matches, training environments, streaming interfaces, collegiate programs, and ethical event design, then build projects that show how competitive play is organized before anyone calls it fair, fun, or professional.
