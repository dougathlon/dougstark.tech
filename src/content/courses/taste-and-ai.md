## Catalog Description

AI and Taste begins from a claim about the humanities in the age of generative models: students need the cultivated capacity to form and justify judgments about style, significance, and value.

The course introduces theories of taste, criticism, and cultural value before turning to systems that simulate, automate, or operationalize judgment. Students experiment with AI tools, build medium-specific criteria, and develop portfolios that present aesthetic judgment as a method for cultural work.
