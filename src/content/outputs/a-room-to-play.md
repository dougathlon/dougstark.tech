"A Room to Play," co-authored with Courtney Rivard, David Hall, and Stephanie Kinzinger, argues that game pedagogy requires more than enthusiasm for play.

Rooms, hardware, libraries, assignments, and maintenance routines determine what kinds of play can become shared evidence. The article treats infrastructure as pedagogy's first argument.
