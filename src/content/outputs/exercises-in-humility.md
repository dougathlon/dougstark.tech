"Exercises in Humility" treats play as an epistemic correction. Bateson's croquet matters because it teaches contingency at the level of conduct, forcing thought to revise itself through failed prediction and bodily adjustment.
"Exercises in Humility: Gregory Bateson on Contingency, Croquet, and Revising Habits of Thought Through Play" appeared in Leonardo 56.1 in 2023.
