"Games as Epistemic Mediators" argues that games do more than make tasks engaging. By moving through Morgenstern, von Neumann, and Bateson, the article shows how games organize uncertainty and make particular forms of reasoning feel actionable.
"Games as Epistemic Mediators: Rethinking Gamification with Morgenstern, Von Neumann, and Bateson" appeared in Configurations 32.2 in 2024.
