Instruction Manuals for Life studies how training formats colonize ordinary life. Farocki's Leben BRD becomes a way to see rehearsed conduct everywhere: in work, bureaucracy, safety, and the managed anticipation of risk.
"Instruction Manuals for Life: Operational Games, Risk, and Continual Training in Farocki's Leben BRD" is a chapter for Depictions of Power: Strategy and Management Games, edited by Simon Dor.
