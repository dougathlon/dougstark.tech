"Ludic Literature" argues that Ready Player One teaches as much as it entertains. The novel turns game literacy into a training regime for neoliberal subject formation: collect, master, upgrade, repeat.
"Ludic Literature: Ready Player One as Didactic Fiction for the Neoliberal Subject" appeared in Playing the Field: Video Games and American Studies, edited by Sascha Pöhlmann and issued by De Gruyter in 2019.
