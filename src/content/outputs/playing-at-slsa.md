"Playing at SLSA" is a co-authored retrospective on building a game studies stream inside a literature, science, and arts conference. The piece treats conference programming as infrastructure for a field still negotiating its methods and publics.
"Playing at SLSA: A Game Studies Stream Retrospective" appeared in Configurations 31.4 in 2023.
