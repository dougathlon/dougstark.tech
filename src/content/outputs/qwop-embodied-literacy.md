"Unsettling Embodied Literacy in QWOP the Walking Simulator" argues that awkwardness is analytic. By making walking absurdly difficult, QWOP exposes the tacit literacies that usually let bodies and interfaces disappear into competence.
"Unsettling Embodied Literacy in QWOP the Walking Simulator" appeared in Journal of Gaming & Virtual Worlds 12.1 in 2020.
