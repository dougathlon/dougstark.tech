"Reimagining Play with Lewis Carroll's Croquet" reads Alice's croquet as a compact theory of unstable play: rules fail, objects misbehave, and the player has to inhabit contingency rather than master it.
"Reimagining Play with Lewis Carroll's Croquet" appeared in In Media Res in March 2020.
