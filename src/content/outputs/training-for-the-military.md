"Training for the Military?" asks why video games so often get explained through training. The article reconstructs historical and media-philosophical contexts in which play becomes a technique for disciplined action.
"Training for the Military? Some Historical Considerations Towards a Media Philosophical Computer Game Philosophy" appeared in Eludamos 11.1 in 2021.
