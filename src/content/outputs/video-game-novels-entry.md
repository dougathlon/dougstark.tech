This encyclopedia entry maps video game novels as a cultural and literary form. It treats the genre as more than fiction about games: a way novels absorb game history, mechanics, development myths, and readerly training.
"Video Game Novels" appeared in the second edition of Encyclopedia of Video Games: The Culture, Technology and Art of Gaming, edited by Mark J. P. Wolf, Greenwood Press, 2021.
