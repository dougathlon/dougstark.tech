Wynter's theory of play treats games and sport as part of the machinery by which genres of the human are made, contested, and lived. The work asks how play can be at once a scene of discipline and a practice for imagining otherwise.

The immediate public form is the 2026 SWPACA paper "Wynter's Theory of Play," now developing toward a fuller article.
